A sperm disorder refers to any condition that affects the production, maturation, motility (movement), or morphology (shape) of sperm cells in males. These disorders can hinder fertility.

A varicocele is a condition characterized by enlarged veins within the scrotum, the pouch of skin that holds the testicles. Varicoceles are similar to varicose veins that occur in the legs.
