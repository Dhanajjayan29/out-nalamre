Fibromyalgia

Fibromyalgia is a chronic disorder characterized by widespread musculoskeletal pain, fatigue, sleep disturbances, and cognitive difficulties.

Causes

  • Genetic predisposition
  • Abnormal pain processing
  • Neurochemical imbalances
  • Sleep disturbances
  • Stress and trauma
  • Hormonal factors
  • Other factors

Symptoms

  • Widespread pain
  • Fatigue
  • Sleep disturbances
  • Cognitive difficulties
  • Other symptoms

Treatment: Modern Medicine

Medications:

  • Pain relievers
  • Antidepressants
  • Anticonvulsants
  • Muscle relaxants

Treatment: Traditional Medicine

  • Heat therapy
  • Cold therapy
  • Herbal supplements
  • Aromatherapy
  • Acupressure and reflexology
  • Dietary changes
  • Mind-body practices

Caution

  • Medication Side Effects
  • Dependency on Medication
  • Avoiding Overexertion
  • Be Cautious with Alternative Therapies

Prevention

  • Maintain a Healthy Lifestyle
  • Manage Stress
  • Prioritize Sleep
  • Seek Prompt Treatment for Injuries and Illnesses
  • Maintain Strong Social Connections
