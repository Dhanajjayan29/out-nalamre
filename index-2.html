Know about heart disease

A heart attack, also known as a myocardial infarction, occurs when the blood flow to a part of the heart is blocked, usually by a blood clot. This blockage deprives the heart muscle of oxygen and nutrients, causing it to be damaged or die if not treated promptly.

Is Boiling water good or bad for health?

Boiling water is generally good for health as it kills harmful bacteria and pathogens, making it safer to drink. It also removes impurities and contaminants, improving its overall quality. However, boiling water excessively can lead to loss of certain beneficial minerals. Overall, moderate boiling of water is beneficial for health.

Does everyone needs 8 hours of sleep?

No, not everyone needs exactly 8 hours of sleep. While 8 hours is often recommended as a guideline for adults, individual sleep needs can vary. Some people may function well with slightly more or less sleep, depending on factors like age, genetics, lifestyle, and overall health. It's important to prioritize quality sleep and listen to your body's cues to determine your optimal sleep duration.
