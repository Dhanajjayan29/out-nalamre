Myths and Facts

Drink 8 Glasses of Water a Day- Fact or Fiction?

Drink 8 Glasses of Water a Day

While drinking 8 glasses is recommended, individual needs vary based on age, weight, and activity level.

Does Sugar Make Kids Hyper?

Sugar Makes Kids Hyper

There is no scientific evidence to support that sugar directly causes hyperactivity in children.

Does Cracking Joints Cause Arthritis?

Cracking Joints Causes Arthritis

Cracking joints is not directly linked to arthritis. The sound is due to gas bubbles in the joints.

Can vaccines cause the flu and autism?

Vaccines can cause the flu and autism

Vaccines are designed to strengthen immunity. Extensive research shows no link between vaccines and autism.
